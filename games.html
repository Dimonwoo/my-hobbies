My Video Games Collection

Crysis

Release date: June 12, 2012

Developer: Crytek

Genre: First Person Shooter

Crysis is a first-person shooter video game series created by Crytek. The series revolves around a group of military protagonists with "nanosuits", technologically advanced suits of armor that give them enhanced physical strength, speed, defense, and cloaking abilities

Call of Duty

Release date: October 29, 2003

Developer: Activision, Infinity Ward, Nokia, Aspyr

Genre: First-person shooter

Call of Duty is a 2003 first-person shooter game developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. It is the first ever Installment in the Call of Duty franchise, released on October 29, 2003, for Microsoft Windows.

Need for Speed

Release date: October 30, 2012

Developer: Criterion Games

Genre: Puzzle

Need for Speed: Most Wanted is an open world racing game developed by Criterion Games and published by Electronic Arts

Sleeping Dogs

Release date: August 14, 2012

Developer: United Front Games, Square Enix Europe, Feral Interactive

Genre: Open World

Sleeping Dogs is played from a third-person perspective. The player controls Wei Shen, a Chinese-American police officer who goes undercover and ventures out on a raid to infiltrate the Sun On Yee Triad organization.

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas

Release date: October 26, 2004

Developer: Rockstar Games

Genre: Open World

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is a 2004 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is the fifth main entry in the Grand Theft Auto series, following 2002's Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, and the seventh installment overall.
